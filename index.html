About Me

Hi, my name is Jessie Sidhu and I'm excited to take this full-stack bootcamp with everyone! I have no coding experience at the moment but i've already learned a lot in the first week!! Picking the Berkeley Bootcamp was probably the best decision I could make as I live in Hercules, CA which is 15 minutes northbound from Berkeley. All the other bootcamps I looked at were all in San Francisco and it just didn't make sense to me to commute that far.

My background for work is in Finance and Business Management. After college, I got a job near St. Louis, Missouri working in Finance and Budgeting; I worked there for about 3 years. I learned a lot but I really missed California! When I got back to California I semi-changed my field of work and got a job in Management working for Tesla as a Production Manager. I did seem to enjoy production a bit more than finance but at the end of the day, I still didn't seem satisfied. In conclusion, I'm really happy I decided to join a bootcamp and do more for myself!
